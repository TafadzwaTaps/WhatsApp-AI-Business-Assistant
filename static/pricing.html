FAQ

Do I need a credit card to start?›
No. The 30-day free trial requires no payment details. You only need to enter payment information when you choose to subscribe.
What happens when the trial ends?›
Your account moves to the free tier with limited features. All your data — products, customers, orders, conversations — is kept safe. You can upgrade at any time to restore full access.
Can I cancel anytime?›
Yes. Cancel any time from Settings → Payments → Manage Subscription. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees.
Do I need a WhatsApp Business account?›
Not on the Trial or Starter plan — you use WaziBot's shared WhatsApp number and your customers reach you through it. On Growth, you can connect your own dedicated WhatsApp Business number at any time.
What currencies do you support?›
Payments are processed in USD via Stripe. Your customers can pay in any currency that Stripe supports in your region. WaziBot's dashboard supports USD, ZWG, and other currencies for your product prices.
Is there a setup fee?›
Never. No setup fees, no hidden costs. The price you see is the price you pay.
