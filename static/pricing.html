Common questions

Do I need a WhatsApp Business API account to start? ›
No. On the Starter and Growth plans you can use WaziBot's shared WhatsApp number instantly — no Meta approval needed. You can connect your own dedicated number at any time once you have your Meta credentials.
What happens after the 14-day trial ends? ›
Your account stays active on the free Starter tier. You keep your products, customers, and order history. Premium features are gated until you upgrade. No data is deleted.
Are there per-message fees? ›
No. WaziBot uses the official Meta WhatsApp Cloud API — no Twilio, no per-message fees on our side. Standard Meta conversation-based pricing may apply on your dedicated number.
Can I cancel anytime? ›
Yes. Cancel anytime from your billing settings. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period.
Does WaziBot support Shona and other African languages? ›
The AI order parser handles Shona, Ndebele, English, and common slang out of the box on all plans. Full multi-language reply translation is available on the Pro plan.
