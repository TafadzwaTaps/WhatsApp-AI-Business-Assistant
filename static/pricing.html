Frequently asked questions

Do I need a credit card to start?›
No. Your 30-day free trial requires zero payment details. You only need to enter payment information when you choose to upgrade to a paid plan.
What happens when my trial ends?›
Your account becomes read-only. All your data — products, customers, orders, conversations — is safely preserved. You can upgrade at any time to restore full access.
Why are prices different in my country?›
WaziBot uses regional pricing to make the platform accessible worldwide. The plans and features are identical — only the price adjusts based on your region. Your price is automatically detected and shown above.
Can I switch between plans?›
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime from Settings → Payments. Upgrades are instant. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
Can I cancel anytime?›
Yes. Cancel from Settings → Payments → Manage Subscription. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, ever.
Do I need my own WhatsApp Business account?›
Not on Trial or Starter — you use WaziBot's shared WhatsApp number. On Growth, you connect your own dedicated WhatsApp Business number for a fully branded experience.
How does Stripe payment work?›
All payments are processed securely by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. WaziBot never stores your card details. You can pay with Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and more depending on your region.
Pricing Notice
Introductory pricing is offered to support our early customers and may be reviewed in the future as WaziBot grows. Prices may be adjusted after the first two months of launch or promotional periods due to increased demand, operating costs, inflation, exchange rate fluctuations, regulatory requirements, or other business factors.

Existing subscribers will be notified in advance of any pricing changes in accordance with applicable laws. WaziBot is committed to keeping pricing fair, transparent, and affordable for businesses of all sizes worldwide.
