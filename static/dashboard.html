ℹ️ How It Works

🛒 Cart Recovery — runs hourly. Customers idle for 60+ minutes receive a WhatsApp nudge linking back to their cart.

🔄 Re-engagement — runs daily. Customers with no orders in 14+ days receive a personalised "we miss you" message.

Both automations are opt-in and respect WhatsApp messaging policies. Messages are sent using your business name.
